About Me

I am an Associate Professor at the School of Computer Science and Engineering at Northeastern University, starting in July 2025. Previously, I was a Research Fellow in the Cyber Security Research Centre at Nanyang Technological University from March 2023 to May 2025, supervised by Prof. Lam Siew Kei. I received my Ph.D. degree from the School of Informatics at Xiamen University in December 2022, supervised by Prof. Cheng Wang. My research focuses on developing intelligent 3D perception systems that enable machines to robustly understand and interact with complex, real-world environments. By leveraging expertise in LiDAR localization and scene reconstruction, I work to translate novel algorithms into practical applications, directly advancing the fields of autonomous systems and robotics.
