About Me

I am an Associate Professor at the School of Computer Science and Engineering at Northeastern University. My research focuses on developing intelligent 3D perception systems that enable machines to robustly understand and interact with complex, real-world environments. This includes LiDAR localization, scene reconstruction, depth estimation, and semantic segmentation.

Openings: I have openings for Master's students (for 2025, 2026 enrollment) and undergraduate interns. If interested, email me.
